## FAQ

### Is online yoga teacher training recognized?
Yes. If the school is a Yoga Alliance Registered Yoga School (RYS), your certification is recognized worldwide. All programs on this list are Yoga Alliance certified.

### Can I actually teach after an online YTT?
Absolutely. Studios, gyms, and online platforms accept RYT-200 credentials regardless of whether you trained online or in-person. Many graduates on this list are teaching full-time.

### How long does it take to complete?
Most self-paced programs give you 6–12 months. Some people finish in 3 months with dedicated study. Cohort programs typically run 3–6 months.

### Do I need yoga experience before enrolling?
Most 200-hour programs accept beginners with at least 6 months of regular practice. You don't need to be advanced — just committed.
