## Canva Design Tips

- **Size:** 1000 x 1500 px (2:3 ratio)
- **Font:** Clean sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins)
- **Colors:** Sage green (#6b7f56), white, warm beige (#f9edd9)
- **Images:** Yoga poses, nature, Bali vibes (use Canva stock photos)
- **Layout:** Title at top (large), subtitle/bullet points below, "yogapathguide.com" at bottom
- **Text overlay:** Keep readable — max 6-8 words per line
